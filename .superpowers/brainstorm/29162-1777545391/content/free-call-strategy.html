Free call strategy — one link or many?

This decision affects every page. The right intake questions matter as much as the structure.

Single link vs separate links

A

One "Free Intro Call" link — used everywhere

One Calendly link. Visitor doesn't have to categorise themselves before booking — that's your job on the call. Intake questions (below) tell you what they need before you pick up.

Best for: Lower friction, simpler to manage, single source of truth for the free call across all pages.

B

Separate links per service type

E.g. "Free Tutoring Consultation", "Free Track Feedback", "Free Studio Enquiry". Visitor self-selects. You get clean analytics on which service drives most enquiries. Can customise confirmation emails per service.

Best for: You want to prepare differently for each call type and track which services are converting.

C

Hybrid — one link, but labelled per context

Same Calendly link everywhere, but the button label changes per page: tutoring page says "Book a Free Tutoring Call", studio page says "Book a Free Studio Enquiry", /free page says "Book a Free Intro Call". One link to maintain, but contextually labelled.

Best for: Simplicity + relevance. Recommended if you're not ready to manage multiple Calendly event types.

Recommended intake questions for Calendly

Regardless of which option you choose — good questions mean you show up prepared and the visitor feels seen before they've even spoken to you.

Question 1 — Required · Dropdown

What are you interested in?

Music tutoring / 1-1 lessons
Studio recording session
Mixing or mastering a track
Not sure yet — just exploring

Why: tells you exactly what the call is about before it happens.

Question 2 — Required · Short text

Tell me briefly what you're working on or what you're trying to achieve

"I've been producing for 2 years, struggling to get my mixes sounding right..."

Why: you can tailor the call completely. They also feel heard before you've even spoken.

Question 3 — Required · Dropdown

Where are you based?

Manchester / nearby — can do in-person
UK — online only
International

Why: immediately tells you if in-person is an option for tutoring / studio.

Question 4 — Optional · Dropdown

How did you find me?

Instagram
Reddit
Google search
Word of mouth / referral
Other

Why: tells you which channels are actually bringing in clients. Gold for marketing decisions.

Question 5 — Optional · Long text

Anything else you want me to know before the call?

Why: catch-all. Some people have a lot to say — give them the space.
