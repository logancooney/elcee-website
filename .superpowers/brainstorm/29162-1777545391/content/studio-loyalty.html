How it appears on the page

A

Separate section below booking — dark, premium feel

After the main booking block and the 3 credibility bullets, a distinct section appears. Different background colour (deep charcoal or near-black with a border) to signal it's a different tier. Headline: "For artists ready to commit." Then the rate, what's included, and a CTA.

B

Small link/toggle below the booking section

Below the booking block, a subtle but clear link: "Serious about your career? See The Residency →" — expands or scrolls to a compact block with the details. Keeps the booking section clean, rewards those who look further.

C

Tab inside the booking block

Two tabs at the top of the booking section: "Book a Session" and "The Residency". Keeps everything in one place. Slightly more interactive — works well if the loyalty plan is something you want equal visibility with the standard rate.
